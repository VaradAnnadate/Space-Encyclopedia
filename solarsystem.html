Mercury:It is the smallest and innermost planet of the Solar System, a dense, rocky "terrestrial" planet with a large iron core and a heavily cratered surface.

Venus: the second planet from the sun and an "Earth's twin" in size and mass, is the hottest planet due to its thick, carbon dioxide rich atmosphere, which creates an intense greenhouse effect, causing surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead

Earth: our home planet, is the third planet from the Sun and the only one in our solar system known to support life. It is a dynamic world, with 71% of its surface covered in liquid water, giving it the nickname "the blue planet".
